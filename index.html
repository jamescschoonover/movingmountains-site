Our Story

Built from nothing.
Building something real.

We're James and Kallan Schoonover — a married couple who decided that debt wasn't going to define our future. Instead of waiting for a way out, we built one.

What started as selling items around the house grew into a multi-platform reselling operation across 7 marketplaces. James drives for Spark and sources inventory. Kallan photographs and prepares every listing. Together, we've built the tools, the process, and the discipline to make it work.

We're not just selling stuff. We're building a business, fighting debt boss by boss, and proving that ordinary families can build extraordinary things when they commit to the grind.
